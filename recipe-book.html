The Perfect Omlette
  1. Prepping Eggs
    • Break 3 eggs into a bowl
    • Add salt and pepper
  2. Prepping Toppings
    • Set aside salt, black pepper, paprika, cayenne pepper
    • Chop onions, bell pepper, spinach
  3. Putting it together
    • Add vegetables to pan
    • Add eggs to to pan
    • Add vegetables on top of eggs
    • Add seasonings along with mozzarella cheese
    • Squeeze outer edges and let eggs cook underneath
    • Fold in half when done and add chives and parsley on top
  4. Et Voila, Enjoy!
